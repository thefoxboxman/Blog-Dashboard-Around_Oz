Exploring Perth and it's surrounds. We spend the best part of a week discovering the exquisite city that is Perth. Then into the Avon Valley exploring for wildflowers, before returning to Fremantle for another five days. Including a day trip to Wadjemup (Rottnest Island) to see the Quokkas and the amazing scenary.
